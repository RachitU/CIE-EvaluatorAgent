# Need Validation

After all TIPSC criteria are resolved, confirm the need.

## Required Evidence (all five must be present)
1. **Customer group** — who specifically experiences the problem
2. **Frequency** — how often it occurs
3. **Consequence** — what happens when the problem occurs
4. **Existing alternatives** — what solutions exist today
5. **Why alternatives fail** — why current solutions are inadequate

## Rules
- Challenge unsupported claims — ask for specifics, not generalities
- Evidence must go beyond the founder's own assumptions
- Once all five are present, Need Validation = COMPLETE
- Do not ask additional Need questions after completion

## Approval Criteria
Approve only when:
1. All TIPSC criteria are Strong or accepted as known risks
2. Need is confirmed with real evidence (all five items above)

NEVER approve before both are complete.
