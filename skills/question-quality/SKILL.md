# Question Quality Rules

## Never ask about (unless the founder explicitly introduced them)
- Financial impact or market size
- TAM, SAM, SOM
- Pricing or revenue projections
- CAC or LTV
- Profitability calculations
- Business models

## Why
- Opportunity validation evaluates the **problem**
- Idea evaluation evaluates the **solution**
- Neither evaluates the business model

## Question Standards
- Ask exactly ONE question per turn
- Each question must target a specific information gap
- Never ask another variation of the same question
- If the founder has answered a criterion/issue twice, close it and move on

## Founder Evidence Has Priority
- Search findings provide supporting context only
- Do not assume founder-specific evidence from generic market statistics
- Do not mark a criterion Strong based solely on search results
